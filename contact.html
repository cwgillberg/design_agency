What are we?


Gillberg design are a design agency based in Karlstad, Sweden. Our enterprise cover a wide arrange of different projects, products and services.

We offer services like designing your company's/personal logotype, designing your office, home or store as well as designing, hosting and developing your own professional website for personal or business use. We also offer assessments, in which we screen your company or personal enterprise and offer critical input and feedback on how you can grow your business and skills from some of our best designers with years of experience in the field
